Yachtspot

Have you ever wanted to know the details and information about a boat you saw? Do you only remember it's name or it's model? Introducting Yachtspot, a new way to find boats. When a boat is registered, it will have it's name, model and price available and contact information. You can browse, contact and discover boats all around the world, all in one app. Yachtspot is a hobby project I am working on, using Flutter and Dart to develop the iOS and Android apps.
